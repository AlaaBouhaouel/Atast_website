About Us

Join the community of excellence!

We are ATAST, a Tunisian association dedicated to the future of science and technology, inspiring and nurturing young scientists with our unique approach. ATAST is a vibrant community where passion for innovation meets comprehensive education, fostering the next generation of STEM leaders.

  • We provide comprehensive teaching programs in robotics, coding, and innovative technologies, designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in today's rapidly evolving technological landscape.
  • Our experienced mentors guide students in developing deep analytical thinking and advanced problem-solving skills, ensuring they are well-prepared to tackle complex challenges and excel in their future endeavors.
  • We offer robust support to foster creative vision, providing the resources and guidance needed to bring innovative ideas to life and achieve tangible results.

Join us at ATAST, where the future of science and technology begins with our youth.
