About SPELL

Spell IT RIGHT ...SAY IT FAST: The 4th Edition of the Tunisian Spelling Bee

ATAST proudly announces the fourth edition of the TUNISIAN SPELLING BEE, the biggest and oldest spelling contest in Tunisia, under the thrilling banner of Spell IT RIGHT ...SAY IT FAST. Divided into five categories—7th & 8th grades, 9th grade, 1st and 2nd grade, 3rd and 4th grade, and University—participants will showcase their spelling prowess through three increasingly challenging rounds, culminating in a high-stakes spelling finale. Each round will be an elimination round, scored based on timing and accuracy, emphasizing both speed and precision. Exciting grand awards await, including a laptop for the first-place winner, a tablet for second place, and a smartphone for third place. Additionally, top contestants will qualify for the international level, with the grand prize being a fully-paid international trip with ATAST.

Get ready to spell your way to success and seize these fantastic opportunities!
